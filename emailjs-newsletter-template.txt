U EmailJS Dashboard:
1. Email Services — poveži Gmail (ili drugi) i zapiši Service ID.
2. Email Templates — kreiraj šablon sa gornjim telom; u Content koristi {{subscriber_email}}.
3. Account → API keys — Public Key (klijentski).
4. U .env.local kopiraj: NEXT_PUBLIC_EMAILJS_PUBLIC_KEY, NEXT_PUBLIC_EMAILJS_SERVICE_ID, NEXT_PUBLIC_EMAILJS_NEWSLETTER_TEMPLATE_ID.

Template ID je u listi šablona (npr. template_xxxxx).
